Severity Definitions

  • Critical Vulnerability can be exploited over the network with no user credentials
  • High Vulnerability can result in compromise of confidentiality or integrity
  • Medium Vulnerability is difficult to exploit or impact is minimized
  • Low Vulnerability has minimal impact or requires unlikely conditions
